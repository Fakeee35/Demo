About Sagar Ratan Foundation

Founded in 2025, we are a community-focused NGO delivering rapid relief in crises and long-term development in education, nutrition, and health access.

Our Mission & Vision

We envision equitable communities where everyone has access to food, healthcare, and learning opportunities. Our mission is to mobilize citizens, partners, and technology to reach underserved populations quickly and compassionately.

SRF implements holistic, community-led interventions focused on:

  • Emergency food & relief distribution
  • Rural & slum health outreach camps
  • Education and digital learning access
  • Livelihood & skills training for families

Our Focus Areas

Targeted programs built with local partners to create lasting change.

Food Security

Meal distribution drives, community kitchens, and nutrition packs for children & seniors.

Health Outreach

Free medical checkup camps, basic medicines, and health awareness workshops.

Education Support

Scholarships, digital learning kits, and community study centers.

Livelihood & Skills

Vocational training and micro-support for women & youth-led enterprises.

Volunteer Network

Mobilizing local volunteers & college youth for rapid ground response.

Tech for Good

Data + mapping to improve last-mile delivery and program transparency.
